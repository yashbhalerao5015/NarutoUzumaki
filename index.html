Strongest Technique

The Rasengan is a jutsu that take form as a spinning ball of chakra, invented by Minato Namikaze.

To perform the Rasengan, users gather chakra in the palm of their hand, which they spin rapidly in multiple directions in order to contain the chakra into a spherical shape

Sage Mode is an empowered state that can be entered by blending natural energy with one's chakra.

In order to enter Sage Mode, the gatherer must remain absolutely still in order to be one with nature and balance the natural energy with their own physical and spiritual energies.
